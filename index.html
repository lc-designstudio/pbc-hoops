About Us

The Patriots Basketball Club is a youth basketball program that focuses on skill development and team play for boys grades 3-8. Our program currently has over 120 players on 15 teams.

The Patriot Basketball Club is the feeder basketball program for Millard South High School

Our goals are to..

  • Teach proper basketball fundamentals to players of all ages.
  • Help players develop individual discipline and team skills.
  • Provide an oppurtunity to play in a competitive atmosphere.
  • Encourage strong leadership skills at an early age.
  • Cultivate behaviors that support personal excellence and responsible citizenship.
  • Promote interest in the Millard South High School basketball program
  • Support the Mission of Millard South High School

Millard South High-school Mission
