Specific areas of team development

The Academy has created a progressive curriculum of drills, stages, and levels so players can work their way up towards mastering certain skills and concepts as they go along, regardless of age or grade.

  • Individual & Team Defense
  • Shooting Form
  • Shooting Off of the Catch
  • Shooting Off of the Dribble
  • Moving Away from the Ball
  • Screening & Reading Screens
  • Passing & Catching
  • Post Moves
  • Individual Moves off the Catch
  • Individual Moves off the Dribble
  • Defensive Transition
  • Proper Rebounding Techniques
