Skills Academy

The age groups are 3rd-4th grade, 5th-6th grade, and 7th-8th grade.

We are proud to offer special skill development sessions coordinated by the Millard South High School Coaching Staff in the spring and summer. The Academy sessions are led by, and curriculum developed by, Millard South High School coaches.

Spring Sessions are held on Wednesday evenings in April and May for 1 hour for each age group.

Summer Sessions are held on Tuesday and Thursday evenings in June and July for 1.5 hours for each age group.

Specific areas of team development

The Academy has created a progressive curriculum of drills, stages, and levels so players can work their way up towards mastering certain skills and concepts as they go along, regardless of age or grade.

  • Individual & Team Defense
  • Shooting Form
  • Shooting Off of the Catch
  • Shooting Off of the Dribble
  • Moving Away from the Ball
  • Screening & Reading Screens
  • Passing & Catching
  • Post Moves
  • Individual Moves off the Catch
  • Individual Moves off the Dribble
  • Defensive Transition
  • Proper Rebounding Techniques
