FAQ's

Do I have to live in the Millard South district to play for the PBC?

We do not limit our teams to ONLY Millard South district youth. However, it is encouraged.

How much does a Season with PBC cost?

If you are new to the Patriot program, registration fees are $450. The $100 submitted at tryouts goes towards this total. If we are not switching uniform designs, returning players can play with the same uniform for $350. Uniform fittings will take place this summer at Prime-Time Sports in Millard. Payment can be made via check or Venmo. All dues need to be paid in full by the start of the season in October.

What is the commitment?

Our basketball season starts with practices in September and goes through early March. Most weeks consist of two 1.5-hour weekday practices and league games or tournaments on the weekend. If your team is playing in a tournament, that "usually" consists of 3 games over the Friday evening - Sunday timeframe. League games are usually one a weekend. We use the Millard South school system's gyms for practices.

How and when will I know if my son is selected?

The offer to play with PBC will be done via email. Only those that are receiving an offer will be emailed. Upon receiving the offer, you will have 24 hours to respond and accept. In most cases, the offer will be for a spot with the club. The level of play your son will be placed at will be determined next fall by the grade's coaching staff. Expect offer emails to be sent 2-3 days after the tryout session.
